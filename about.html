We create a diverging stacked bar chart to plot a 5 point Likert scale.

There a lots of ways to plot a Likert scale but according to Robbins & Heiberger, a diverging stacked bar chart is the best and we agree.

We've used D3.js. There is also an R package by Robbins & Heiberger.

The original can be found at github forked from wpoely86's block: Diverging Stacked Bar Chart
